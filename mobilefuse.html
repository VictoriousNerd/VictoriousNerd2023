MobileFuse

 Advertising Agency

By: Victor Morse

MobileFuse is a mobile advertising services company based in New York, NY and Waltham, MA. MobileFuse's specialties are the metrics, distribution, and ad creation with a focus on mobile environments. While at MobileFuse I was a User Interface and User Experience Designer as well as a Mobile Advertising Designer. On the advertising side I created Landing Pages that were desktop and mobile friendly, as well as a variety of Rich Media Ads for smartphones and tablets.
