JamHub

 Music Equipment
2012 – 2014
By: Victor Morse

JamHub was a small startup in the music equipment industry. They specialized in unique technological solutions that allowed musicians to play music together more often. Their flagship products, the JamHub studios, allowed multiple musicians to connect to a single device and with personal headphones have mixes for each musician. While at JamHub I created a vast library of work in their marketing department and in product and app development.

The BandLab Beta app (under UI/UX Design) was created to generate interest for investors. The product sold during my time at JamHub and is now one of the industries leading DAWs (Digital Audio Workstation).
