WindTalker Security

 Information Security
2017 – 2018
By: Victor Morse | Additional Credits: David Hatch, Founder, Principal at CMO in Residence

WindTalker Security was a company in the information security field that I freelanced for. Their products offered strong encryption for sensitive documents for industries like law and health professionals. The company was in a state of growth and needed a designer that has solid design and development skills. I designed and maintain the current WindTalker website, as well as a variety of marketing documentation and templates.

WindTalker sold in 2023 as a result of all the new branding and marketing work that went into the company.
