Hobbies & Interests

Design is more than my profession. It is also a passion of mine. And like all artists, I love to occasionally mix my passions. These are a mix of old school projects and personal projects that I really enjoyed creating.

The VGL posters are a re-creation of an old college project. A personal favorite I had to revisit it years later after my skills had developed further. Video Games Live is a concert series that performs video game music with a classical orchestra. Find out more on their site, videogameslive.com.

Play Free is a fictional event created for a group project. I really enjoy this piece because it challenged how the participants viewed video games. Video games have grown up, but how? And how have they not?

My skate posters are a complete fan piece. Brian Shima and Jeff Stockwell are two of my favorite aggressive inline skaters. What is aggressive inline skating? The short, it’s an old X-Games sport that refuses to die. The long answer is in this Wikipedia article.

The New Bedford Mercury Typeface is a typeface I created for a school project. I started with an old newspaper from the 1800’s. I zoomed in on letters that had been distorted in interesting ways by the printing process. I then took these printing mistakes and built them up into a typographic form.
