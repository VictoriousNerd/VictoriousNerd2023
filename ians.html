IANS

 Information Security
2015 – 2017
By: Victor Morse | Additional Credits: Dave Hatch, Catherine Riggi, Aaron Shi

IANS is short for the Institute for Applied Network Security. They are an information consultancy firm that helps large institutions make informed security decisions. While at IANS I was responsible for bringing a modern style to the company. This began with a new website and followed by numerous branding projects from documents to PowerPoints.

Since information security can be a difficult topic to explain to lay-people I created numerous brand-specific graphics and icons that the entire company could used to help explain complex InfoSec ideas and topics.

During my time at IANS Research I created a new visual language that is still used by the company today. I also helped bring in numerous projects significantly under their budgets.
