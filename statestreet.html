State Street

 Finance
2018
By: Victor Morse | Additional Credits: Mark Boroyan, UX Director

I was part of a new team that was being built within State Street. We were tasked with creating a new centralized system for all of State Street's current and new products. Our department's primary goal was creating lo- and hi-fidelity wireframes based on customer requests. We used a pre-set design language created by State Street's internal branding team. We used Sketch and Axure to create low-fidelity mockups and hi-fidelity prototypes.

While working for State Street I also completed an IBM Design Thinking course. This course taught us valuable skills and methodologies to consistently design great products to solve their intended needs.
