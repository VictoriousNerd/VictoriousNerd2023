Websites

The must-have asset of the 21st century. If your business doesn't have a website, you may as well kiss it goodbye. From small start-ups, to large corporations; it is imperative to have a well-designed, and adaptive site. The adaptive part is especially important in current times. The number of users viewing content on mobile devices is growing by leaps and bounds.

I have strong development skills that I couple with my experienced design skills to create amazing websites that function well, adapt, and will make your clients take notice. I've worked with a variety of CMS (Content Management Systems) and site sizes. I've designed and built basic WordPress sites for small start-ups to large corporate sites based on proprietary, or enterprise level systems.
