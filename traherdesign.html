Traher Design

 Graphic Design Agency
2009 – 2013
By: Victor Morse | Additional Credits: Elissa Traher

Over the past several years I’ve been a contract designer for Traher Design, a boutique design studio located in historic Lexington, MA. The principle designer, Elissa, hired me for my expertise in web and ability to nail tough designs. Over the years I’ve worked on several projects for Traher Design, most notably the design and build of their portfolio website.

Sometimes Elissa would bring me in for my advanced development skills. On projects like the NXP Newsletter template and the Otto Laske I was able to take her designs and translate them to effective, working code.
